# Release Announcement — v1.0

We are pleased to announce the v1.0 release of the Machine Readable AI Legislation Feed.

This release delivers a stable, jurisdiction-aligned dataset covering **United States, United Kingdom, and European Union** legislative and regulatory signals relevant to artificial intelligence, digital systems, and data governance.

## What This Release Includes

- Curated, machine-readable metadata for AI-relevant legislation and regulatory instruments
- Jurisdiction-specific repositories for:
  - United States
  - United Kingdom
  - European Union
- A locked and versioned metadata schema
- Conservative relevance classification for AI, digital, and data-related instruments
- Immutable historical archives and a clearly defined `latest.json` pointer

## Stability & Governance

- Schema frozen under a formal Schema Lock Declaration
- All datasets validated against a shared release checklist
- Archive records preserved immutably
- Version v1.0 represents a complete and stable snapshot as of the release cutoff date

## Licensing & Compliance

- US data: Public domain (US Government works)
- UK data: Open Government Licence v3.0
- EU data: © European Union, reused under the EU Open Data Directive and EUR-Lex reuse policy

Only metadata, summaries, and source links are distributed.  
Full legislative texts are not redistributed.

## Intended Use

This release is suitable for:
- Regulatory monitoring systems
- Compliance tooling
- AI governance workflows
- Research and analytics
- Commercial and enterprise integration

## Version Status

- Version: v1.0
- Status: Frozen
- Next updates will be released under v1.x or v2.0 according to schema governance rules
