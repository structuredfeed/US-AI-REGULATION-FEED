# Commercial Use Guide

This dataset is designed for enterprise integration.

## Overview
The AI Regulatory Intelligence Feed is a structured, machine-readable dataset designed to help organizations monitor and manage legal and regulatory developments related to artificial intelligence, digital systems, and data governance.

It transforms complex public-sector legislation into clean, versioned, automation-ready metadata suitable for enterprise workflows.

## What You Get

- Curated regulatory signals from official government sources
- Structured JSON feeds aligned across jurisdictions
- Clear relevance indicators for AI, digital, and data governance
- Immutable historical archives and stable “latest” pointers
- Jurisdiction-specific separation for clean compliance boundaries

## Jurisdictions Covered (v1.0)

- United States (Federal)
- United Kingdom
- European Union

## Supported Uses
- Internal compliance systems
- Risk monitoring
- Regulatory dashboards
- AI governance platforms

## Integration
- Drop-in JSON
- Stable schema
- Backward-compatible updates

## Support Model
- Schema stability guaranteed post-v1.0
- Jurisdictional expansion via new SKUs

Each jurisdiction is maintained in a dedicated repository with aligned structure and governance.

## Designed For

- Compliance and legal monitoring teams
- AI governance and risk management
- Policy research and regulatory intelligence
- Product and platform teams building AI-aware systems
- Vendors integrating regulatory signals into tooling or dashboards

## Data Integrity & Trust

- Sources are exclusively official government publishers
- No scraping, inference, or unofficial aggregation
- Metadata fidelity preserved exactly as published
- Conservative classification rules to avoid overreach

## Licensing & Reuse

This product distributes **metadata only**.

- Original code, schemas, and classification logic are MIT licensed
- Public-sector source material is reused under applicable open data regimes
- No copyrighted legislative text is redistributed

This structure enables:
- Commercial reuse
- Internal enterprise deployment
- Downstream licensing under MIT / Apache-2.0 / CC-BY-4.0 compatible models

## Schema Stability

- A locked schema governs all v1.x releases
- Breaking changes require a major version bump (v2.0)
- Buyers can rely on long-term structural stability

## Update Model

- Rolling updates when new qualifying instruments are published
- No artificial churn
- Stable snapshots via tagged releases
