# Product SKU Catalog

## SKU-US
- Scope: United States
- Coverage: Federal legislation, regulations, regulatory agency outputs
- Sources: Congress.gov, Federal Register, official agency publications
- License: Public domain sources + MIT for metadata
- Intended customers: US-based organizations

## SKU-UK
- Scope: United Kingdom
- Coverage: Acts, Statutory Instruments, retained EU law, regulators
- Sources: legislation.gov.uk, ICO, DSIT, CMA
- License: OGL v3.0 + MIT for metadata
- Intended customers: UK-based organizations

## SKU-EU
- Scope: European Union
- Coverage: Regulations, Directives, delegated & implementing acts
- Sources: EUR-Lex, legislation.gov.uk (retained EU law mirrors)
- License: EU Open Data Directive + MIT for metadata
- Intended customers: EU and multinational organizations

## SKU-GLOBAL
- Scope: US + UK + EU
- Coverage: All above, harmonized schema
- Intended customers: Multinational enterprises
- Delivery: Unified feed or region-separated feeds
